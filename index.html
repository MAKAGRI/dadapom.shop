About Us

NY Queens Unplugged LLC curates cozy, high-fidelity acoustic sessions and stripped-down live shows. We handle pop-ups, private events, brand activations, and recordings with a focus on emotion, storytelling, and a great audience experience.

Intimate: Small-format sets that foreground the artist.
Mobile: Pop-up stage and modular audio anywhere.
Sonic Quality: Pro mics, careful gain staging, and warm mixes.
Turnkey: Booking, backline, audio crew, and content capture.
